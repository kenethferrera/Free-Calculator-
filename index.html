💡 Frequently Asked Questions

💰 What happens if I win a game?

You receive the payout listed for your tier (e.g., $10, $20, or $30) directly into your wallet. Payouts apply to Tiers 1–3 only.

🔄 What happens if I draw a game?

You’ll get 1 game credit refunded, based on how you originally played the match:

  • Paid Credit: Refund is reusable or cashable.
  • Ad-Earned Credit: Refund can only be reused for playing.
  • Brutal Tier: No refunds (free tier).

🤑 Can I cash out refunded credits?

Yes, if they were from paid games.
No, if from ad-earned games — they’re play-only.

🎯 What’s the difference between paid and ad-earned credits?

Type Cost Refundable for Cash? Usable for Gameplay?
💳 Paid Credit $0.20 ✅ Yes ✅ Yes
📺 Ad Credit Watch 1 ad ❌ No ✅ Yes

🔁 Can I play unlimited games using ads?

  • ⏳ 1 ad = 1 credit every 30 minutes
  • 📅 Max 5 ads per day
  • 🚫 Max 2 ad-earned games in a row (then you must play 1 paid game)

📉 What happens if I lose?

No payout and no refund.

📊 Can I track my stats and credits?

Yes! Your dashboard shows:

  • Total games played (by tier)
  • Win/Draw/Loss record
  • Wallet balance & earnings
  • Paid vs ad-earned credits
  • Ad usage history
